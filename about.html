Who we are

Our story, mission & vision

Our story

Born from passion for esports.

Founded in June 2025, AL Management was built with a simple ambition: giving talented players access to the best opportunities. Driven by agents with five years of experience, we place our clients' interests at the heart of every decision.

Our mission

Accompany. Create. Elevate.

Our mission is to accompany players every day — creating opportunities and providing everything required to reach their full potential, on and off the server.

Our vision

A reference on the world stage.

We aim to become a true reference and pivotal hub across EA FC and Fortnite — a place where the best organizations come to find the talent that will define tomorrow's esport.

Our journey

How we got here

June 2025
Foundation of AL Management

AL Management is created with an exclusive focus on EA FC and a strong francophone identity. The agency signs its first players and begins building its network from the ground up.

February 2026
International expansion on EA FC

The agency opens its doors to international players while preserving its roots. New partnerships with European clubs are established, marking a turning point for the agency.

March 2026
Launch of the Fortnite division

AL Management enters the Fortnite competitive scene, signing its first EMEA players and extending its expertise to a second major title.

June 2026
International growth of the Fortnite division

Three months after launch, the division expands internationally — welcoming players from multiple countries and establishing AL Management as a truly global agency across two titles.
