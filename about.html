Our story

Born from passion for esports.

Founded in June 2025, AL Management is an esports management agency built with a simple ambition: helping talented players access the best opportunities. Driven by agents with more than five years of experience in the industry, we place the interests and ambitions of our clients at the heart of every decision.

Our mission

Accompany. Create. Elevate.

Our mission is to accompany players at every stage of their career — day after day, challenge after challenge. We create the opportunities they need and give them everything they require to reach their full potential, on and off the server.

Our vision

A reference on the international stage.

We aim to become a true reference and a pivotal hub across the EA FC and Fortnite competitive scenes — a place where the best organizations come to find the talent that will define tomorrow's esport.

Our journey

How we got here

June 2025
Foundation of AL Management

AL Management is created with an exclusive focus on EA FC and a strong francophone identity. The agency signs its first players and begins building its network.

February 2026
International expansion on EA FC

The agency opens its doors to international players on EA FC while preserving its francophone roots. New partnerships with European clubs are established.

March 2026
Launch of the Fortnite division

AL Management enters the Fortnite competitive scene, signing its first players in the EMEA region and extending its expertise to a second major title.

June 2026
International growth of the Fortnite division

Three months after launch, the Fortnite division expands internationally — welcoming players from multiple countries and establishing AL Management as a truly international agency.
