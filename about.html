AL Management

Built on passion, driven by ambition. Discover our story.

Founded in June 2025, AL Management is an esports management agency built with a simple ambition: helping talented players access the best opportunities.

Driven by passionate and dedicated agents with more than five years of experience in the esports industry, our agency places the interests and ambitions of its clients at the heart of every decision.

Like the players we represent, we are a growing organization driven by the desire to improve, innovate, and reach the highest levels of competition.

At AL Management, we build tomorrow's success today.
